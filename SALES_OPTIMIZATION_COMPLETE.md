## 💡 KEY LEARNINGS

### What Works:
1. ✅ **Social proof everywhere** (trust)
2. ✅ **Show, don't tell** (demos, code)
3. ✅ **Remove risk** (guarantee, trial)
4. ✅ **Specific value** (ROI calculator)
5. ✅ **Competitive edge** (comparison table)
6. ✅ **Content marketing** (SEO blog)
7. ✅ **Urgency** (sticky bar, scarcity)

### What Doesn't Work:
1. ❌ Generic claims ("best", "fastest")
2. ❌ Too much text (people scan)
3. ❌ Hidden pricing (transparency wins)
4. ❌ Complex explanations (simple wins)
5. ❌ No social proof (people need it)
